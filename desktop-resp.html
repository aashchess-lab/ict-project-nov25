School Awareness

Creating responsible citizens

  1. Problems & Simple Solutions
    • Litter on the Ground
      Good Habit: Always use the nearest bin.
      Solution: Put more bins and create small clean-up teams.
    • Loud Noise in Hallways
      Good Habit: Walk and talk quietly.
      Solution: Add “Quiet Zone” signs and remind others politely.
    • Food and Wrappers After Break
      Good Habit: Clean your desk before leaving.
      Solution: Start a “Clean Desk Routine” and reward the cleanest class.
    • Pushing Furniture
      Good Habit: Lift chairs gently.
      Solution: Add felt pads on chair legs and teach safe moving rules.
